The minimum account opening documentation required for individual accounts are: one passport photograph, valid utility bill and a valid means of identification (international passport, drivers licence or national ID card).

The minimum account opening documentation required for corporate accounts are: certificate of incorporation, forms CO2 and CO7, MeMat, passport photograph of the directors, utility bill, board resolution to open account, and signature mandate.
